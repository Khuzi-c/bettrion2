Signs of Problem Gambling

You may have a gambling problem if you:

  • Spend more money than you can afford to lose
  • Chase losses by gambling more
  • Lie to family/friends about gambling
  • Feel anxious or irritable when not gambling
  • Neglect work, school, or relationships
  • Borrow money to gamble

Self-Help Tools

Most casinos offer responsible gambling tools:

  • Deposit Limits: Set daily/weekly/monthly limits
  • Time Limits: Restrict session duration
  • Self-Exclusion: Temporarily or permanently ban yourself
  • Reality Checks: Reminders of time spent gambling

Tips for Safe Gambling

  • Only gamble with money you can afford to lose
  • Set a budget before you start
  • Never chase losses
  • Take regular breaks
  • Don't gamble when emotional or intoxicated
  • Keep gambling as entertainment, not income
