Understanding Gambling Laws

Online gambling legality varies by country and region. Laws may cover:

  • Whether online gambling is permitted
  • Minimum age requirements (18, 19, 21, etc.)
  • Licensed vs unlicensed operators
  • Tax obligations on winnings
  • Restricted payment methods
